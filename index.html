About Us

At the heart of our operations is a commitment to excellence

PT Adijaya Teknindo is a medium-sized Structural Construction & Maintenance Contracting company located in Balikpapan, East Kalimantan.We focus on providing construction, fabrication and maintenance services for a wide range of industries,including the mining, and oil & gas industries operating in Kalimantan and across the Indonesia Archipelago.

From initial design and fabrication to the final stages of maintenance, PT Adijaya Teknindo is dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of our clients with precision and expertise.
